# 🎧 Audiogeek

Audiogeek is an open-source application that helps listeners discover the deeper story behind the music they love.

The app analyzes the tracks you listen to and provides rich contextual information about them — including artist insights, musical influences, recommended playlists of similar-sounding tracks, and the original songs that may have been sampled.

Audiogeek is designed for music enthusiasts, crate-diggers, and curious listeners who want to go beyond the surface of a song and explore the wider musical ecosystem around it.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎵 Track Intelligence

Get contextual information about the music you listen to:

- Artist background
- Album and release information
- Genre and stylistic influences
- Historical context

### 🔎 Sampling Discovery

Explore the lineage of your favourite songs:

- Tracks that were sampled in the song
- Songs that later sampled the track
- Original source recordings

### 🎧 Smart Recommendations

Discover new music based on sound similarity:

- Playlist generation based on track characteristics
- Similar artists and songs
- Deep catalog discovery

### 📚 Musical Context

Learn about the cultural and sonic influences that shaped a track:

- Genre evolution
- Influential artists
- Production styles

### ⚡ Fast & Lightweight

Audiogeek is built to be responsive and efficient while delivering rich music insights.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

Audiogeek combines multiple data sources and audio analysis techniques to surface relevant musical relationships.

The app:

1. Analyzes track metadata and audio characteristics
2. Queries music knowledge databases
3. Identifies stylistic similarities between tracks
4. Maps sampling relationships
5. Generates discovery playlists

The goal is to make music exploration **intuitive, educational, and fun**.
